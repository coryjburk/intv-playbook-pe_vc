Executive Interview Battlecards

Must-Know PE Concepts

  • Arbitrage Mechanics: Understanding purchase multiples vs exit multiples leverage impact.
  • Deleveraging: Using historical free cash flows to systematically minimize senior debt.
  • Cash-on-Cash Return: Quick MOIC translation relative to traditional IRR calculations over time horizons.

Investment Power Phrases

  • "Let's look at this asset through a structural margin preservation lens..."
  • "What are the non-cyclical structural tailwinds driving organic unit economic health?"
  • "I want to unpack the recurring revenue visibility and contract stickiness profiles."

Metrics To Know Cold

  • Net Working Capital: Current Assets (ex Cash) minus Current Liabilities (ex Debt).
  • Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR): EBITDA divided by Total Debt Service Obligations.
  • Free Cash Flow Conversion: (EBITDA - CapEx) divided by EBITDA.

LBO Mental Shortcuts

  • A 2.0x MOIC over exactly 5 years yields an approximate IRR of 15%.
  • A 3.0x MOIC over exactly 5 years yields an approximate IRR of 25%.
  • Doubling your equity investment over a 3-year holding horizon yields roughly 26% IRR.

Value Creation Levers

  • Cross-Selling Velocity: Maximizing wallet share across historical under-penetrated accounts.
  • SGA Rationalization: Stripping redundant corporate overhead through shared service models.
  • Tech Enablement: Migrating legacy custom frameworks over to unified scalable platforms.

Transformation Language

  • "Designing an operating rhythm that matches our standard execution pacing blueprint."
  • "De-risking post-merger systems integration paths through early blueprint alignment."
  • "Driving functional accountability metrics down directly to shop floor personnel tiers."

First 100-Day Priorities

  • Secure alignment on key performance criteria models across the business leadership suite.
  • Establish baseline visibility rules into transactional working capital run-rates.
  • Isolate immediate low-friction EBITDA enhancement adjustments within purchasing vectors.

Executive Communication

  • Lead with the macroeconomic enterprise outcome before diving deep into micro-operational mechanics.
  • Contextualize metric variations against pre-established investment thesis underwriting parameters.
  • Present direct actionable binary decisions paths alongside your operational recommendations.
